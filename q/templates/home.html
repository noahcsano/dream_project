Research Form Procedure

In order to create a Google Form for your research project, follow these steps:

  1. Select the topics or questions you want to include in your form.
  2. Log in to your Google account and go to Google Forms.
  3. Click on the '+' button to create a new form.
  4. Give your form a title and description.
  5. Add your selected topics/questions as form fields (e.g., multiple-choice, text, etc.).
  6. Customize the design and settings of your form as needed.
  7. Preview your form to ensure everything looks correct.
  8. Once satisfied, click on the 'Send' button to distribute your form via email, social media, or direct link.
  9. Collect and analyze the responses to your research questions!
